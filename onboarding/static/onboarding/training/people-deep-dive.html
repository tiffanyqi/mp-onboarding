Deep Dive into People vs Engagement/People Reports

People vs. Engagement

People data allow you to study and engage your users based off their current, up-to-date profile. Event data allow you to study your users based off each of their actions, and their individual, evolving characteristics, over time.

  • Why are these two separate?
    • What do we usually say to customers (i.e. what are the benefits of keeping them separate?)
    • It has to do a lot with how the platform is created, that gap is going to shrink and shrink and shrink until it’s gone
  • What are the biggest customer pain points that come up with regards to this?
    • “Can I see a list of users who did an event” (yes)
    • “Can I send a notification to users who did an event X number of times?” (no)
    • “Transactional notifications" (Can’t do that based on the signup, but you can store as a people profile property)
  • Setting People Properties as Super Properties

  • Seeing People who did certain events
    • Why track this? Explore can be useful to gather a list of users based off Events that they have triggered/milestones they have accomplished across their lifecycle. You can also send Event-based Notifications if you track these Events as People properties.
