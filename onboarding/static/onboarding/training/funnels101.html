Funnels 101

About

The Funnels report allows you to optimize processes on your site or app to drive users to convert. You can use this report to track your key user flows, see your step-by-step and overall conversion rate and discover ways to improve conversion. Our Funnels report is completely retroactive unlike many of our competitors meaning you can create custom funnels and view historical conversion on the fly.

The Mixpanel Funnels report is best in breed. This session will help you understand the value of the Funnels report, how to build a report, and how we differentiate from our competitors.

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Explain what the Funnels report can do.
  • Explain how the Funnels report is calculated.
  • Be able to give consultative feedback based on funnels.

The Funnels report tracks unique users moving through key flows on an app or site. Funnels help customers improve their product flows to provide a better user experience.
