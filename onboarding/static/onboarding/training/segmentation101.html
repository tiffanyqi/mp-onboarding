Segmentation 101

About

Segmentation is the key report that customers use in Mixpanel. This session will help you understand the value of segmentation, why we use it, and how the segmentation report is structured.

Segmentation allows you to view top events on your app and easily breakdown complex events in Mixpanel. You have the ability to drill down by an unlimited number of properties to gather instant insight into these key actions on your app. You can choose any individual event in Mixpanel, compare multiple events and see total events, unique users firing these events and average number of events per user. We also give you multiple options to view this data.
