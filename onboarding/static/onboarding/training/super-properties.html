Super Properties Super Stars

About

Super properties often take an implementation from good to great. Collecting super properties for user demographics, user actions, and feature usage allow our customers to make actionable product decisions based on Mixpanel.

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Explain what Super Properties are, and why they exist
  • Explain the different ways customer uses super properties.
  • Explain the difference between a super property and regular property.
