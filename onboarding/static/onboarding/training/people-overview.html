People Reports Overview

About

Our People product allows you to track your individual users with custom user profiles and send targeted notifications based on their use of your application.

Mixpanel Engagement (our analytics reports) is the main product that most customers come to Mixpanel for. Our other product is Mixpanel People, which stores unique user profiles and allows customers to create notifications targeted at specific users.

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Understand the People product and how to speak to customers about it.
  • Understand the basic capabilities of People reports and tools.
