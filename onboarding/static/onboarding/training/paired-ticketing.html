Tips to Start Tickets

When you do live tickets, you won't know how to do most of them, and that's ok. Here's the general procedure for when you don't know how to answer something:

  • Check the IKB.
  • Search through public-facing documentation.
  • Look at past tickets on how someone's solved a similar inquiry.
  • Ask within your team.
  • Ask in DFTQ!
