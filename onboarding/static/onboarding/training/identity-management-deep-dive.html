Deep Dive into Identity Management

Guiding Questions

What is the role/significance of distinct_id?

  • Explain to a customer who is implementing Mixpanel for the first time, what is distinct_id?
  • In what reports does it play a significant role?
  • What happens when all users have the same distinct_id?
  • What happens if a user’s distinct_id changes across their lifecycle?
    • How might this happen?
    • If someone calls identify if you haven’t called alias

Managing identity - the methods

  • What does the alias call do? Allows you to link pre-authentication activity to post-authentication activity using an alias table - specific to your project. Searches through table, finds that “bob” is just a placeholder for 123.
    • Does the distinct_id change with the alias call?
  • What does the identify call do? Changes the distinct_id, and creates a profile (links people.set activity to profile)
    • Does the distinct_id change if you are calling identify on the original distinct_id? Only if you don’t call alias first
    • When would you call it on the original distinct_id, and when would you call it on a different distinct_id?)
    • What else does it do?
    • Note: Trainees seem to grasp the concept of alias, but we need to do a better job of making sure they also understand the role and importance of the identify as a standalone method
  • What is the “best practice” for using the alias and identify calls?
    • Why?
  • Alias table lives in Mixpanel in the back end...you can check if a specific value is an existing alias via mixpanel.com/admin/internal. Alias tables are specific to projects and cannot be exported.

Tripwires and booby traps around identity management

  • New Hire: Define an orphaned profile?
  • What is a race condition?

    • How does one avoid a race condition?
  • Cross platform tracking
    • Do you send information to separate projects, or the same one?

  • Use cases, edge cases
    • “How should I handle my identity management system when I already have pre-existing users who have already signed up and therefore won’t be aliased?”
    • “How do I prepare my app for multiple users to create accounts on the same (read shared family iPad) device?”
