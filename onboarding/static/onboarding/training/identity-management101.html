About

Mixpanel is a platform agnostic analytics tool providing the ability to track individual users across multiple devices. We do this by assigning each user a unique ID called the distinct_id which persists in the browserâ€™s cookie on web and the local storage of the device on mobile.

The way Mixpanel handles identity management is different than many other analytics tools and is one of the most common technical conversations you will have with customers.

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Successfully apply concepts of identity management to real life customer examples.
  • Communicate the basics of identity management to a customer.
  • Understand the basic calls involved in managing identity on Mixpanel.
