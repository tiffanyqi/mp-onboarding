Notifications 101

About

Notifications allow you to build and automate recurring notifications from Mixpanel. Options include email, SMS, push, webhook, in-app. Our notifications are targeted based on People properties so that you can reach out to specific segments of your user base on an ongoing basis.

We also provide full analytics on your notifications so that you can see how notifications impact recipient actions in Segmentation, Funnels and all Engagement reports.

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Build a notification campaign for each notification type.
  • Know the difference between one-time and recurring campaigns.
  • Provide the information Mixpanel needs to create each of the different types of notifications.
  • Understand the difference between Notifications targeting via People properties versus via Events and Event properties, and the limitations of both
