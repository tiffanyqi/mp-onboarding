Your Official Trainings Are Done!

By this point, most (if not all) of your official trainings are complete. congratulations!

However, this doesn't mean you're done learning (you'll #AlwaysBeLearning), so please feel free to continue to ask questions and learn as much as you can. You can go back to the previous weeks to review milestones and objectives. Also review Reference and Trainings for everything you've learned before.

As you finish up onboarding around the 6th week, ensure that you have done the following:

  1. Finished all Practice Tickets on Salesforce (and reviewed each one with a member of the team)
  2. Attended at least 3 paired ticketing sessions
  3. Attended at least 2 call shadowings
  4. Completed Music Finder
  5. Completed implementation spec
  6. Populated a dungeon master
  7. Completed demo call with mentor and/or manager
  8. Schedule 2 webinar dry-runs with your mentor before you host the webinar
  9. Completed all Support Onboarding Sessions Feedback Forms
  10. Have at least met 1 person from every single team outside of Support, (ask Mentors or teammates for intros!)

Objectives

Your first week will be spent primarily acclimating with the team and familiarising yourself with Mixpanel - the product, the services we provide, the day-to-day functions of the organisation, and of course, the people that make working here so awesome!

You will also have a variety of projects and milestones that you will be working towards every week. Below are a few to help you get started in becoming a fully ramped-up Support Engineer:

Training Sessions

  • Full attention
  • Take notes! There’s a lot of ground to cover so you’ll be doing your brain a service if you take notes in each session

Music Finder

  • Create a Music Finder folder
  • Download Sublime
  • Configure Music Finder to run locally

Implementation Spec

Pick a company of interest (anything you want!), and after reading this guide, start considering:
  • What business objectives matter most to that business?
  • What kind of questions should I ask to improve the business's ability to reach those objectives?
  • How can tracking analytics help answer those questions?
  • How can Mixpanel's specific analytics philosophy/design help answer those questions?

Webinar

  • Watch the webinar and take notes!
  • What doesn’t seem clear about the webinar?
  • What’s disconnected?
    • You will begin hosting the webinar near week 5+ (or further out - they are held bi-monthly on Tuesdays at 11am. Have questions? Ask Ehsan!)
    • To watch the webinar, register on the intro help page here and make your way to Herbie ~15 minutes before the webinar starts

Meet people!

  • The more people you meet, the better time you’ll have! It’s simple. :)

Implementation Spec

  • Build out an implementation spec for the company you’ve chosen
  • Include events, triggers, properties, super properties, people properties.
  • Justify how and why each event/property applies to solving a product or business goal.
